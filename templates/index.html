Common Phishing Examples

Learn to recognize common phishing attempts by examining these examples. Click on any example to see detailed analysis.

Bank Alert Scam

From: security@yourbank.com

Subject: Urgent: Unusual Login Attempt on Your Account

"We've detected a login attempt from a new device. If this wasn't you, please verify your account immediately by clicking the link below."

Red Flags: Urgent language, Generic greeting, Suspicious link, Requests for immediate action
Package Delivery Scam

From: no-reply@delivery-service.com

Subject: Your Package Could Not Be Delivered

"We couldn't deliver your package due to an incorrect address. Click here to update your delivery information."

Red Flags: Unexpected delivery notice, Requests for personal information, Suspicious sender email
Account Verification Scam

From: support@netflix-account.com

Subject: Action Required: Your Account Will Be Suspended

"We've detected unusual activity on your account. Verify your payment information now to prevent account suspension."

Red Flags: Threat of account suspension, Requests for payment info, Sender's email doesn't match official domain
